With a touch of sweetness and a Caribbean flair, this seasoning adds a little kick to your favorite meats. This paprika-based blend has notes of allspice, nutmeg and sage plus sugar for a nice caramelized finish. Try rubbing on pork chops for the grill or the pan. Also use on ribs and chicken.

Hand-mixed from: paprika, sea salt, sugar, black pepper, allspice, nutmeg, crushed red pepper, sage and white pepper.
