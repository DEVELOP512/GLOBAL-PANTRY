Delightfully garlicky and earthy with just the right amount of sea salt, this blend adds a little zestiness to beans, soups, stews, eggs, chicken and pork. The citrus in this blend balances the touch of chipotle chile heat.

Hand-mixed from: Chipotle chile, smoked paprika, garlic, onion, black pepper, cumin, lemon zest, orange zest, sea salt, cayenne, crushed red pepper flakes and Mexican oregano.

Net weight: 2.4 oz
