This versatile curry powder blends the flavors of Madras for a balanced sweetness of cinnamon, cardamom and fenugreek and a touch of cayenne heat.

Use in recipes calling for curry powder or in marinades with lime or lemon. Add pizzazz to tuna salad or roasted potatoes.

Hand-mixed from: Turmeric, coriander, mustard powder, fenugreek seed, cumin, ginger, black pepper, cinnamon, kashmiri chile, cardamom and cloves. (Salt-free)
