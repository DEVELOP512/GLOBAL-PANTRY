Brought to the West Indies by Indian workers, Jamaican curry powder distinguishes itself from Indian styles with the addition of allspice. Use in your favorite curry recipe or try an authentic Jamaican goat or chicken curry.

Hand-mixed from: Turmeric, coriander, allspice, garlic, ginger, fenugreek seed, cayenne, black pepper, cinnamon, cumin, thyme, star anise, anise seed and cloves. (Salt-free)
